About This Map

Virginia has 11 districts. The Virginia Redistricting Commission, a bipartisan committee made of 8 legislators and 8 citizens. The districting map was originally made by the commission, which deadlocked in agreement. Ultimately, the job was passed onto the Virginia Supreme Court, leading to a less gerrymandered version.

  • Districts are divided by lines into 11 regions.
  • Color shading on the left side panel can be changed to view the districts in different categores like partisan lean or by varying color.
  • Hover over each district to see demographics for those specific districts. Click on Statistics and Analyze to see a breakdown and rating for the districting based on the demographics
